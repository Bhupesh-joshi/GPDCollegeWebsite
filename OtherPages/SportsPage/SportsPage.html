Annual sports Report (2019-20)

With the purpose to develop sportsman spirit among students and to enhance their physical and social skills, a three day annual sports meet 2019-20, was organized from 13/02/2020 to 15/02/2020 at institutional playground of G.P.Dwarahat.

The function was inaugurated by the principal madam,[ Mrs. Rachana Singh]. In his inaugural speech he appreciated the students for their punctuality and their interest in sports activities. Inaugural function ended after the vote of thanks by the H.O.D(I.T.) [Mr.Jagdish Chandra Pandey].

Thereafter student actively participated in various indoor and outdoor games with full sportsmanship. The valedictory function was held on the 3rd day of annual sports meet. After that the institutional principal, congratulated the winners and honored them awards and certificates. The following is the list of students, who got prize in different games-
